A cozy little mobile web app for catching cats in the wild.

Spot a cat, sign in with Google, and take its photo. The app checks that there's really a cat in the shot, then Gemini paints a warm, lofi, retro-anime style portrait of it — added straight to your personal collection. Every cat you catch keeps a streak going, and each one can be shared or compared against the original photo.
